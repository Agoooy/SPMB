<h1><strong>New Student Registration System</strong></h1>

<p>This project is a Laravel-based application specifically designed to assist colleges and schools in the student admission process. Featuring an intuitive interface and comprehensive features, the application allows prospective students to easily and efficiently apply online.</p>

<hr>

<h2><strong>Features</strong></h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Online Registration:</strong> Prospective students can fill out the registration form online with personal details, educational history, and other information.</li>
  <li><strong>Document Upload:</strong> Facility to upload important documents such as transcripts and identification papers.</li>
  <li><strong>Online Examination System:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Ability to create and schedule online exams with various question types such as multiple choice.</li>
      <li>Tracking of exam time and time limits for each exam session.</li>
      <li>Automatic grading of answers and generation of exam reports for review by the committee or administrator.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Health Check:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Online health check form for prospective students including medical history, symptoms, and other important health information.</li>
      <li>Capability to upload relevant medical documents such as lab test results and medical records.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Online Interviews:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Form for conducting online interviews between prospective students and the committee.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li><strong>Notifications:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Robust notification system to inform prospective students about their registration status.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2><strong>Tech Stack</strong></h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Client:</strong> Tailwind CSS, Vue.js, Inertia.js, Vite</li>
  <li><strong>Server:</strong> Laravel, MySQL</li>
</ul>
